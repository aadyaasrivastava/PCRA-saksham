About Us

Petroleum Conservation Research Association (PCRA), a society under the Ministry of Petroleum Natural Gas, with the help of public sector Oil and Gas companies organizes a one-month conservation campaign “Sanrakshan Kshamata Mahotsav (SAKSHAM)”.

The tagline of SAKSHAM-2021 is

“Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav through Green & Clean Energy”

OBJECTIVE

The objective of SAKSHAM is to impress the masses for adopting fuel conservation and efficiency measures for environment protection. During this campaign, many group activities are conducted pan India. Communication through Social Media Platforms/Television/Radio, Print Media Publications etc. for effective dissemination of the message of fuel efficiency and conservation.
